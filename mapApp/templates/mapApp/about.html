About

Bikemaps.org is run by the Spatial Pattern Analysis and Research Lab (SPAR Lab) out of the University of Victoria's Department of Geography. We're interested in collecting cycling data to research spatial patterns between infrastructure and cycling accidents, thefts, and hazards in an effort to help better inform cycling infrastructure decisions. The data you send to us, which will always remain anonymous, ultimately helps to improve safety and infrastructure for cyclists.
